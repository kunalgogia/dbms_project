Animal Cemetery

"If having a soul means being able to feel love
and loyalty and gratitude, then animals are
better off than a lot of humans."
James Herriot

At ISVHCC, we understand the grief of losing a companion animal and offer one of Delhi's
only burial grounds for animals. Graves are freshly dug and the animal laid to rest in
the presence of the owner.A fee is charged for this service. Space allowing, ISVHCC plans
to offer saplings to owners to plant in memory of their companion animal.

The donation helps us give free treatment to needy animals and
you and your pet grants a new lease of life to other animals.

We also encourage you to adopt another animal. The best way to remember your pet
is to open your heart and home to another and relive the joy of sharing and caring.
When you are ready to adopt another animal, ISVHCC is glad to help you choose
from among its many animals. A homeless animal would get a good home and you'd
get a best friend for free.
