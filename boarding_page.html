Boarding


Innocent Souls Veterinary Hospital and Care Centre provides premium boarding facility for your pets. Pet parents can entrust their pooches and cats with us. ISVHCC has the highest safety and comfort standards, and your pets' best interests in mind

Our cage-free service allows for your dog and/or cat to roam about and play in our secure, pet-friendly play area, and be shifted to their cages only during nap/night time. We separate our animals based on size and species to ensure comfort for all. We walk, feed and play with your pet.
Other services can also be booked such as

  • Private kennels
  • Special treats
  • Grooming services
  • Extra walks/playtime
  • Special medical care

Our 24x7 supervised kennels, flexible and healthy meal plans, drop-off and pick-up facility and unconditional love ensure the absolute best for your pet!

Please make sure that your pet is up to date on its vaccinations and that you bring along their vaccination card and micro-chip ID.
