LABORATORY


A small outbreak of disease can pose a threat to the entire animal community. Thus, to ensure the overall wellbeing of animals, the practice of veterinary diagnostics becomes essential. The veterinary diagnostics (pathology and radiology) lab at Innocent Souls Veterinary Hospital And Care Centre is equipped with quality instruments, apparatuses, accessories and offers quality diagnostic services for veterinarians and owners of livestock and companion animals in the form of routine diagnostics and toxicology for animal species.
