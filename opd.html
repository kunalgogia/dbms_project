OUT PATIENT DEPARTMENT

Our outpatient department treats over 200-300 cases a day. Apart from providing treatment,
doctors counsel owners about the diet to be given to their pets and they also give suggestions on
their exercise and hygiene. There is a seperate vaccination center to minimise the risk of
infection  The OPD is open from morning 9am to evening 5pm. 
We also provide 24-hour ambulance service under the OPD itself.
